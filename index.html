Welcome To Surrey's Newest Jiu-Jitsu Gym

Our academy, situated at the Surrey Martial Arts Club in Redhill, Surrey, stands as the premier destination for Brazilian Jiu Jitsu (BJJ) in the wider Surrey area, including Redhill and Reigate.

Our extensive timetable features BJJ and No-Gi/Submission Grappling classes offered seven days a week, catering to individuals of all ages and skill levels. Our team of eight BJJ black belt instructors, known for their unwavering passion for imparting the art of Brazilian Jiu Jitsu, have achieved remarkable success both as instructors and competitors. Their expertise is exemplified by the numerous titles they and their students have earned in renowned events such as IBJJF, European, British, English, NAGA, and regional competitions. To learn more about our instructors, please refer to our website.
